<p><p>The worm gear market is segmented into three main types: single envelope, double envelope, and non-enveloping worm gears. Single envelope worm gears consist of a single helical gear wrapped around the worm, offering high torque transmission and compact design. Double envelope variants have two helical gears providing improved efficiency and load capacity. Non-enveloping worm gears, characterized by a distinct non-helical design, allow for smoother operation at lower speeds. Each type caters to specific industrial applications based on performance needs and space constraints.</p></p>
