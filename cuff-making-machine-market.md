<p><p>The cuff making machine market is primarily divided into two types: the PE plastic sleeve machine and the non-woven cuff machine. The PE plastic sleeve machine manufactures cuffs using polyethylene, suitable for disposable applications in healthcare and food industries due to their waterproof and hygienic properties. In contrast, the non-woven cuff machine produces cuffs from non-woven textiles, offering breathability and comfort, making them ideal for medical garments and protective clothing. Both machines cater to growing demands for protective apparel.</p></p>
