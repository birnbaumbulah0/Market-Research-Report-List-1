<p><strong>The Floor-mounted Radiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor-mounted radiography market comprises two main types: CR (Computed Radiography) Tech Digital X-Ray Systems and DR (Digital Radiography) Tech Digital X-Ray Systems. CR systems utilize a phosphor plate to capture images, which are later processed digitally. They are generally more cost-effective and adaptable to existing equipment. In contrast, DR systems employ flat-panel detectors for immediate image capture, offering enhanced image quality and faster processing. Each type caters to varying clinical needs and budgets, impacting their adoption in healthcare settings.</p></p>
