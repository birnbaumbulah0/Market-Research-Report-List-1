<p><strong>The Bioprosthetic Heart Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porcine Aortic Tissue Valve</li><li>Bovine Pericardial Tissue Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bioprosthetic heart valve market is primarily categorized into two types: porcine aortic tissue valves and bovine pericardial tissue valves. Porcine aortic tissue valves are derived from pig heart tissue, offering excellent durability and a natural feel, making them suitable for many patients. Bovine pericardial tissue valves, created from cow pericardial tissue, provide a flexible and robust option, often with a lower risk of calcification. Both types are preferred for their biocompatibility and reduced need for long-term anticoagulation therapy.</p></p>
