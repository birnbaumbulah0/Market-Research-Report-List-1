<p><p>Sealers are categorized into three market types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual sealers require human operation for each sealing task, offering flexibility but lower efficiency. Semi-automatic sealers streamline the process by automating certain functions while still needing operator involvement, enhancing productivity. Fully automatic sealers operate independently, handling the entire sealing process without human intervention, ideal for high-volume production environments. Each type caters to different business needs, from small-scale to large industrial applications.</p></p>
