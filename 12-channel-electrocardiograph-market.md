<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter ECG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 12-channel electrocardiograph market encompasses several types of ECG monitoring, each serving distinct purposes. Resting ECG analyzes heart activity at rest, providing essential baseline data. Stress ECG evaluates cardiac response during physical exertion, aiding in diagnosing conditions like ischemia. Holter ECG involves continuous monitoring over 24 hours or more, capturing irregularities in daily activities. Together, these modalities enhance cardiovascular assessment, enabling timely diagnosis and management of various heart conditions, ultimately improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
