<p><p>Epoxy bonding agents are categorized by viscosity: low, medium, and high. Low-viscosity agents flow easily, ideal for penetrating fine cracks and porous surfaces, ensuring deep adhesion. Medium-viscosity agents balance flow and strength, suitable for general-purpose bonding and filling moderate gaps. High-viscosity agents are thick, providing robust structural support and filling larger voids, often used in heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific needs, offering tailored solutions for adhesion, durability, and performance across diverse industries like construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
