In contrast, Large-Tow Carbon Fiber employs fewer, thicker filaments, resulting in cost-effectiveness and efficient production for larger turbine blades. Both types enhance turbine performance, contributing to improved energy efficiency and reduced environmental impact in wind power generation.</p></p>
