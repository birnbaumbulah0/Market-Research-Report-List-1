<p><strong>The Deionized Water System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-bed Deionized Water System</li><li>Mixed-bed Deionized Water System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deionized water system market primarily comprises two main types: the two-bed deionized water system and the mixed-bed deionized water system. The two-bed system utilizes separate cation and anion exchange units to remove ions, making it more suitable for applications requiring moderate water purity. In contrast, the mixed-bed system combines both cation and anion exchange resins in a single vessel, delivering higher purity levels of deionized water, making it ideal for critical applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals and electronics.</p></p>
