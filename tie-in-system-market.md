<p><p>Tie-in systems in the market are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their integration structure. Vertical tie-in systems involve a single company controlling multiple levels of production or distribution within the same industry, ensuring cohesive operational efficiency. In contrast, horizontal tie-in systems involve partnerships or alliances among companies at the same production level, enhancing market reach and sharing resources.
