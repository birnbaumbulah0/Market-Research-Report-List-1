<p><p>An LED light box is a display device that uses LED lights to illuminate graphics, advertisements, or artwork, creating a visually appealing and attention-grabbing effect. LED light boxes are widely used in retail, advertising, hospitality, and corporate environments due to their energy efficiency, durability, and vibrant visual impact.</p><p>The LED Light Box Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
