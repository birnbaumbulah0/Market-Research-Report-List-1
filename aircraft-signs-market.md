<p><p>The aircraft signs market is divided into two main types: static and dynamic. Static signs are fixed installations that convey essential information, such as exit directions or safety instructions, using materials like metal or plastic. Dynamic signs, on the other hand, are electronic displays that can change messages, providing real-time information about flight status, gate changes, or emergency announcements. Together, these sign types enhance passenger navigation and safety within airports, adapting to different informational needs.</p></p>
