<p><strong>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edge Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) market is primarily categorized into two types: Edge Type and Direct Type. Edge Type BLUs position LEDs along the panel's edges, providing light that spreads across the display through a light guide. This design is slim, making it ideal for ultra-thin devices. In contrast, Direct Type BLUs place LEDs directly behind the LCD panel, offering uniform brightness and better color accuracy, though they are bulkier. The choice between these types depends on design requirements and desired display performance.</p></p>
