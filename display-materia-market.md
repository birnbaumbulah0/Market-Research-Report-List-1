<p><p>The display materials market is primarily categorized into two types: LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode). LCD technology uses a backlight and liquid crystals to produce images, making it suitable for applications like TVs and monitors due to its cost-effectiveness. Conversely, OLED technology offers superior contrast, vibrant colors, and flexibility, as each pixel emits its own light, leading to thinner designs. While LCD remains prevalent, OLED is gaining traction for premium devices, enhancing user experience and applications.</p></p>
