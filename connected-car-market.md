<p><strong>The Connected Car Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded solutions</li><li>Integrated solutions</li><li>Tethered solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Connected Car Market** comprises three primary types:  </p><p>1. **Embedded Solutions**: Vehicles with built-in connectivity hardware and software, enabling independent internet access for features like navigation, diagnostics, and real-time updates without external devices.  </p><p>2. **Integrated Solutions**: Systems that connect smartphones to the car's infotainment system, leveraging the phone's connectivity for apps, calls, and media while integrating with the car's display and controls.  </p><p>3. **Tethered Solutions**: Use external devices (e.g., dongles) to provide connectivity, linking the car to the internet via smartphones or other external networks for basic telematics and data sharing.</p></p>
