<p><p>Flexographic printing inks are categorized mainly into four types: </p><p>1. **Water-Based Inks**: Environmentally friendly, these inks are versatile and widely used for packaging materials, offering good adhesion and quick drying.</p><p>2. **Solvent-Based Inks**: These inks provide excellent color vibrancy and are suitable for non-porous surfaces but may have environmental concerns due to VOC emissions.</p><p>3. **UV Cured Inks**: These inks cure instantly under UV light, providing high durability and resistance, ideal for various substrates.</p><p>4. **Other Inks**: This category includes niche formulations, such as hybrid inks that combine properties from the above types for specialized applications.</p></p>
