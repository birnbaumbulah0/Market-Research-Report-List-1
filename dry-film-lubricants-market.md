<p><ul><li>PTFE-Based Dry Film Lubricants</li><li>Molybdenum Disulfide-Based Dry Film Lubricants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry film lubricants are solid coatings applied to surfaces to reduce friction and wear without the need for liquid oils. PTFE-based dry film lubricants utilize polytetrafluoroethylene, offering excellent chemical resistance, low friction, and smooth performance in high-temperature environments. Molybdenum disulfide-based dry film lubricants use MoS2, providing superior load-bearing capacity and performance in extreme pressure and vacuum conditions. Both types are widely used in automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications, offering durability and reliability in challenging operating conditions.</p></p>
