<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle ignition coil market includes two main types: single-spark and multi-spark systems. Single-spark ignition coils deliver one spark per ignition cycle, commonly used in traditional engines for reliable performance. Multi-spark ignition coils generate multiple sparks within a single cycle, enhancing combustion efficiency, reducing emissions, and improving fuel economy. Multi-spark systems are increasingly adopted in modern vehicles for their advanced performance, while single-spark systems remain prevalent in older or cost-sensitive models. Both types cater to varying engine designs and performance requirements.</p></p>
