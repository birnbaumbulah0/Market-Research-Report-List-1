<p><p>The eye glitter market is categorized into three main types: glitter flakes, glitter gels, and glitter dust. Glitter flakes offer a chunky texture that adds dramatic sparkle, often used for bold looks. Glitter gels provide a smooth, adhesive base, allowing for easier application and a more blended effect, ideal for a subtle shimmer. Glitter dust consists of fine particles, perfect for a light, ethereal glow, and is often used for highlighting or accentuating specific areas around the eyes.</p></p>
