<p><ul><li>DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communication)</li><li>Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless communication technologies for automotive applications, such as DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communication) and Mesh networks, enable vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) and vehicle-to-infrastructure (V2I) connectivity. DSRC operates on a dedicated frequency band, offering low-latency, high-reliability communication for safety-critical applications like collision avoidance and traffic management. Mesh networks, on the other hand, create decentralized, self-healing connections among vehicles and infrastructure, enhancing coverage and scalability for real-time data sharing. Both technologies aim to improve road safety, traffic efficiency, and support emerging autonomous driving systems.</p></p>
