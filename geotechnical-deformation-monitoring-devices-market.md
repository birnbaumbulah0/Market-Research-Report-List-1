<p><p>Geotechnical deformation monitoring devices are essential for assessing ground stability and structural integrity. The market comprises two main types: geodetic measuring devices, which utilize techniques like GPS and total stations to track large-scale land movements with high precision; and geotechnical measuring devices, which focus on in-situ monitoring of soil and rock properties using instruments such as inclinometers, piezometers, and strain gauges. Both types provide critical data for construction, mining, and environmental management projects.</p></p>
