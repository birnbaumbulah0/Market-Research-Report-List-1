<p><p>Anhydrous ammonia fertilizer is a concentrated nitrogen-based fertilizer widely used in agriculture to enhance crop yields. It contains 82% nitrogen, making it one of the most efficient sources of nitrogen for plants. It is applied directly into the soil, where it reacts with moisture to release nitrogen, which is essential for plant growth. Due to its high nitrogen content and cost-effectiveness, it is a popular choice among farmers, particularly for large-scale farming operations.</p><p>The Anhydrous Ammonia Fertilizer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. However, challenges such as volatility in raw material prices and environmental concerns related to ammonia emissions may impact growth.
