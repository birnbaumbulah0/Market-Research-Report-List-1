<p><p>Natural preservatives are substances derived from natural sources, such as plants, animals, or microorganisms, used to extend the shelf life of food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals by inhibiting microbial growth and oxidation. The cosmetics and personal care industry is also witnessing significant growth, as consumers seek safer and eco-friendly alternatives.</p><p>Emerging trends include the development of innovative natural preservatives with enhanced efficacy, such as fermented ingredients and plant-based antimicrobials. Additionally, advancements in extraction technologies and the use of biotechnology are expanding the application scope of natural preservatives.
