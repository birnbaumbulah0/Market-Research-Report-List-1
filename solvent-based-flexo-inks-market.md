<p><p>The solvent-based flexo inks market can be categorized into two primary types: compostable and non-compostable inks. Compostable solvent-based flexo inks are designed to break down naturally after disposal, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for sustainable packaging solutions. In contrast, non-compostable solvent-based flexo inks are traditional formulations that do not decompose naturally, often containing petrochemical materials. These inks are widely used in various applications but raise environmental concerns due to their lasting impact on ecosystems.</p></p>
