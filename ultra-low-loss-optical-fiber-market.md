<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra low-loss optical fiber market is primarily segmented into two types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber allows multiple light modes to propagate, making it suitable for short-distance communications and high-bandwidth applications within data centers. Conversely, single-mode fiber supports a single light path, ideal for long-distance transmissions with minimal signal loss, making it essential for telecommunications and WANs. Both types are crucial for enhancing data transmission efficiency and supporting modern communication networks.</p></p>
