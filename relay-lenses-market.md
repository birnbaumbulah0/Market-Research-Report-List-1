<p><p>The Relay Lenses market is primarily divided into Ordinary Spherical Lenses and Aspheric Lenses. Ordinary Spherical Lenses have uniform curvature and are widely used in various optical applications due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Aspheric Lenses feature a non-spherical surface profile, which minimizes optical aberrations and enhances image quality, making them ideal for advanced imaging systems and high-performance devices. Both types serve distinct needs, driving innovation and efficiency in optical designs.</p></p>
