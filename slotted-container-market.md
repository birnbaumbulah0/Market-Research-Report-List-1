<p><p>The slotted container market encompasses various designs tailored for specific packaging needs. Half Slotted Containers (HSC) have flaps only on one end, making them ideal for display or easy access. Regular Slotted Containers (RSC) feature flaps on both ends, providing strength and versatility for shipping. Overlap Slotted Containers (OSC) have flaps that overlap by a significant margin, enhancing their load-bearing capacity. Each type serves distinct purposes in storage, shipping, and retail, catering to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
