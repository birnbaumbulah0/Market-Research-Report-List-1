<p><strong>The Gel-Filled Fiber Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gel-filled fiber cable market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode and multi-mode fibers. Single-mode fibers are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss and improve bandwidth. In contrast, multi-mode fibers support multiple light pathways, making them ideal for shorter distances and high-speed data transfer within buildings. Both types incorporate gel as a protective element, enhancing performance by preventing moisture and providing stability in various environmental conditions.</p></p>
