<p><p>The wrapping machine market consists of three main types: Manual (or Hand) Wrapping Machines, Semi-Automatic Wrapping Machines, and Automatic Wrapping Machines. Manual wrapping machines require operators to wrap products by hand, offering flexibility and low investment for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic machines combine manual loading with automated wrapping, enhancing efficiency for medium-sized applications. Automatic wrapping machines fully automate the process, offering high-speed and consistent wrapping suitable for large-scale production, making them ideal for industrial applications.</p></p>
