<p><strong>The Aluminum Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Molding Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum casting market comprises several types, each with distinct methods and applications. Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into molds, offering precision and smooth finishes, ideal for high-volume production. Permanent molding casting uses reusable molds for enhanced durability and quality in medium production runs. Sand casting, the most traditional method, utilizes sand molds, allowing for complex shapes and lower production costs, suitable for small batches. Other methods include investment casting and gravity casting, expanding the versatility of aluminum casting applications.</p></p>
