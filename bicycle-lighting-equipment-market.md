<p><p>The bicycle lighting equipment market consists of three main types: Halogen lights, Xenon lights, and LED lights. Halogen lights are traditional incandescent bulbs known for their bright, warm glow but are less energy-efficient. Xenon lights offer a brighter output than Halogen, providing enhanced visibility, though they can be more expensive. LED lights are the most popular choice due to their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and various brightness settings, making them ideal for modern cycling needs and safety.</p></p>
