<p><p>The E-Scooter Helmet market features two main types: full face helmets and open face helmets. Full face helmets provide comprehensive protection by covering the entire head, including the chin and jaw, making them ideal for high-speed rides or urban commuting. Open face helmets, on the other hand, offer more ventilation and a greater field of vision, making them popular for casual rides but with less coverage. Each type balances safety and comfort, catering to different rider preferences and needs.</p></p>
