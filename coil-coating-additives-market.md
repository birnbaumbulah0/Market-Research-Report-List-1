<p><strong>The Coil Coating Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coil coating additives are classified primarily into solventborne and waterborne types, each offering distinct benefits for metal surfaces. Solventborne additives use organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and durability but can pose environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. In contrast, waterborne additives utilize water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and safer to use. They often require additional drying time but deliver lower emissions and easier cleanup, appealing to manufacturers seeking sustainability alongside performance in coil coating processes.</p></p>
