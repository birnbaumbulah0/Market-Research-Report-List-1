<p><p>Hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs) are a class of synthetic compounds primarily used as refrigerants, foam-blowing agents, and solvents. Governments globally are implementing stricter regulations to curb greenhouse gas emissions, further pushing industries to adopt sustainable practices.
